## Collet
- **Type**: Artifact
- **Subtype**: Jewel
- **Colors**: colorless
- **Mechanic**: attaches to a creature like the colored jewels; the
  creature gains a tap-activated ability that can pay for one HAND
  or GRAVEYARD cost component on a cast (parallel to the colored
  jewel cycle: `azure-jewel`, `blue-jewel`, `brown-jewel`,
  `black-jewel`, etc.). Color-share rule for the substitution is
  open — `collet` being colorless, the natural reading is that it
  pays for any HAND/GY component without a color requirement.
- "Collet" is the setting/claw in jewelry that holds a stone — the
  empty mount that takes any gem.
